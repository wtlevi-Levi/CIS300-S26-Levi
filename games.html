Nintendo's Heavy Hitters

The House That Mario Built

It should come as no surprise that Mario games are often some of the best-selling games on each Nintendo platform. The series has been a cornerstone of Nintendo's success for decades, with each new installment reaching millions of fans worldwide. Although the series started off as with platforming gameplay, the Mario Kart games have become so popular that the spin-off series often outsells the mainline titles!

The Success of Tie-In Games

A tie-in game is a game that is sold as a part of a bundle that includes a game console. Every single best selling game featured in the list below was offered as a tie-in game at some point during each console's lifecycle. Selling games as a part of these bundles has been a successful strategy for boosting game sales, with Wii Sports being one of the most famous examples of a tie-in game. Due to the massive success of the Wii as a console, Wii Sports became one of the best-selling games of all time due to the amount of bundles that were sold!

What's Next?

With the recent launch of the Nintendo Switch 2, it is uncertain what Nintendo's next best selling game will be. With numerous popular franchises like Mario, Pokémon, and more, the future looks bright for Nintendo's gaming lineup!
