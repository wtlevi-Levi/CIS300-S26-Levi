Nintendo's Numerous Franchises

Super Mario

Millions of people around the world recognize Mario even if they don't play videogames. With well over 100 games in the series, Mario has become the face of Nintendo and one of the most iconic characters in gaming history. Whether you are playing one of his platforming adventures, popular racing games, or a sports spin-off, Mario games are known for their casual gameplay that anyone can enjoy.

Pokémon

Starting off as a simple monster collecting game, Pokémon has evolved into a global brand that includes video games, trading cards, animated series, and more. The series is known for its strategic gameplay and trading system that allows players to swap out Pokémon in their collection. In the first generation of Pokémon games, there were 151 total Pokémon to collect. Now, there are over 1000 Pokémon to capture and train across dozens of games!

The Legend of Zelda

The Legend of Zelda is a beloved action-adventure franchise that has been captivating players for decades. Known for its expansive worlds, intricate puzzles, and memorable characters, the series has become synonymous with high-quality gaming experiences. From the original The Legend of Zelda to modern titles like The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, the franchise continues to push the boundaries of what is possible in action-adventure games.
